Acting

Acting classes can be a great way for parents to help their children learn about acting, and develop their skills.

Many kids have an interest in acting, but don’t know how to get started. Acting classes offer a safe space for kids to explore their own creativity and imagination, while also learning valuable skills that will serve them well throughout life.

Our acting classes are often fun and exciting, providing an opportunity for kids to learn about different aspects of the industry. They’ll learn about the business side of things, as well as the technical aspects of acting such as performance techniques and character development.

Parents who want to get involved with their children’s acting classes should do some research on what kind of classes are available near them. Some schools offer drop-in acting classes while others require formal auditioning before admission is granted. If you don’t find anything nearby, try looking online at sites like Stage Door or Understudy Magazine that list upcoming auditions in your area.
